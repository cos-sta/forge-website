Process

How Forge ISO Works

1

Create Your Listing

Fill in the job details — title, description, requirements, pay rate, location, and number of slots. It takes just a few minutes.

2

Workers Apply

Your job is listed in the marketplace. Workers browse, filter, and apply to jobs that match their skills and availability.

3

Review & Accept

Review applicant profiles, check ratings and trade skills, then accept the best candidates for the job.

4

Coordinate & Complete

Message accepted workers to share details. After the job, leave reviews to build your network for future positions.
