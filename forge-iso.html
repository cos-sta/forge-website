Features

Quality You Can Verify

Every feature in Forge ISO is designed to give clients confidence and give builders a platform to prove their worth.

🛡

ISO-Verified Profiles

Builders upload their ISO certifications, trade licences, and insurance documents. We verify them so clients can hire with peace of mind.

🔍

Search & Filter

Find the right builder for your project. Filter by trade, location, certification level, ratings, and availability to narrow down your options.

⭐

Reviews & Ratings

Every completed project generates a review. Over time, the best builders rise to the top with a track record that speaks for itself.

📝

Project Listings

Clients post project briefs with scope, budget, timeline, and location. Qualified builders review the details and submit their interest.

💬

Direct Messaging

Once you have found the right builder, coordinate directly through the platform. Discuss project details, timelines, and expectations in one place.

👤

Builder Portfolios

Builders showcase past projects, certifications, and specialisations. Clients can review their work history before making a decision.
