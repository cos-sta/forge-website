Features

Built for Urgency

Every feature in Forge INSTANT is designed to get workers on-site as fast as possible.

📍

Live Radar Search

When you post an ASAP job, Forge scans for available workers near your location in real time with a live radar animation so you can see the search happening.

⏱

Flexible Deadlines

Choose from ASAP (4 hours), Today (10 hours), Tomorrow (24 hours), or 2 Days (48 hours). The urgency level determines how the matching algorithm prioritises your job.

👤

Sequential Offers

Workers receive offers one at a time with a 30-second response window. This ensures fair allocation and avoids overbooking. You see progress as each candidate is contacted.

💬

Instant Messaging

Once a worker accepts, you're connected via in-app messaging to coordinate arrival, share site details, and manage the job without leaving the app.

🏠

Trade Selection

Select the exact trade you need from a curated list of construction and shift work roles. Your request goes directly to workers with matching skills.

💰

Set Your Rate

Choose your pay rate per hour or per day with a simple slider. Workers see the rate upfront so there are no surprises when they accept.

Process

How Forge INSTANT Works

1

Select Your Trade

Open Forge INSTANT and choose the type of worker you need from the trade list. One tap and you're moving.

2

Set Pay & Deadline

Slide to set your pay rate and choose your urgency level. ASAP triggers the real-time dispatch system.

3

Swipe to Request

Confirm with a swipe gesture. Your location is auto-detected, and nearby workers start receiving offers immediately.

4

Worker Accepts

Watch in real-time as candidates are contacted. Once someone accepts, you're connected and ready to coordinate.
